We champion the Hacker Ethos.

The word "Hacker" describes those who are passionate about solving problems and overcoming limitations.

"Any job can be hacked. You can be a carpenter hacker. It doesn't have to be high-tech. It has to do with craftsmanship and being dedicated to what you're doing."

— Quoted from The Hacker Ethic and the Spirit of the Information Age
