Closing Remarks

We embrace the hacker spirit

The term 'Hacker' describes those who are passionate about solving problems and overcoming limitations.

"Anyone can be a hacker in their profession. You could be a carpenter hacker. It's not necessarily about high technology. As long as it involves skill and you're deeply focused on what you're doing, you can be a hacker."

Source: "Hackers: Heroes of the Computer Revolution"

We also advocate the GNU philosophy, believing that openness leads to collective progress

We promote lifelong learning—continuous accumulation of knowledge enriches life, regardless of professional context

INGO (International Non-Governmental Organization) & INPO (International Non-Profit Organization) & GNU|OS (GNU Philosophy | Open Source Co-creation) & CL (Continuous Learning)

We are a group of individuals pursuing our dreams
