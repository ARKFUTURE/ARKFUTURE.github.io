We embrace the Hacker Spirit

The term Hacker is used to describe those who are passionate about solving problems and overcoming limitations.

"Any profession can be a hacker. You can be a carpenter hacker. It doesn't have to be high-tech. As long as it involves skill and you are deeply focused on what you are doing, you can be a hacker."

Quoted from "The Hacker Ethic and the Spirit of the Information Age"

We also embrace the GNU spirit, believing that openness enables collective progress

We advocate lifelong learning — continuous accumulation enriches your life, whether working or not

We are a group of people pursuing our dreams
